      GROUP CLASSES

      At SWEAT Strength + Cycling, we do “Circuit-Based” HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) Strength classes. This means we move from station to station, with each “station” being a different exercise. We generally alternate periods of intense exercise with active (but lower-intensity) recovery periods. Every class is different, so you’ll never be bored.


      We offer Strength & Fusion classes. STRENGTH classes shock your body using dumbbells, kettle bells, TRX-style suspension, body-weight, and plyometrics for the entire workout. FUSION classes combine indoor cycling intervals with the strength circuit – exercising both your cardiovascular and muscular systems!


      We also offer MATURE MUSCLE classes for beginners, mature bodies, and those who have taken a long absence from exercise. This low impact class is perfect for individuals who are interested in building strength without the impact and intensity of our HIIT classes.


      Every class is a sweaty, upbeat and energizing workout designed to fatigue your muscles, raise your heart-rate and body temperature, and boost your resting metabolism for up to 24 hours afterwards. The circuit allows you to work against your own fitness baseline, and class dynamics help foster a camaraderie that encourages you to set and reach your individual fitness goals.


      We also provide free towel service (you’re gonna need it, trust us), and sell high quality post-workout protein shakes in a variety of flavors to complement the progress you make in the studio.
