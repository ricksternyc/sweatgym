SWEAT Strength + Cycling is a boutique group fitness and private training studio. Our mission is to make our community happier, healthier, and leaner through fun, challenging, high-energy workouts.

Our in-store retail shop offers a variety of new collections and styles each month. In addition to our performance gear and lifestyle wear for outside the studio, we also collaborate with top-rated partner brands like ALO, Tasc Performance and Bella.

We have two locations:

SWEAT Oakland is in the Rockridge Shopping District (just five blocks south of the BART station) SWEAT Albany is on Solano Avenue (across the street from Safeway)
