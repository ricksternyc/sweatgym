      PRIVATE TRAINING

      Need more attention for faster results? Or perhaps you’d like a private session in order to familiarize yourself with the exercises and the HIIT methodology?


      Get individual training and nutrition advice specific to your goals with one-on-one sessions – or bring a partner to share in the cost.

      If you want a little more personal attention, but still want to take group classes, check out our sweet intensive – a personal fitness and lifestyle program that includes:

      • Unlimited classes
      • Daily protein shakes
      • Individual diet plans and monitoring
      • Tracking of personal goals and metrics
      • Before and after "WOW" photos

      How it works: Meet with one of the program leaders to discuss your goals, take measurements and photos, and decide on a specific time frame for your program. You can then take as many classes as you like (most folks do between 3 to 6 a week), and track your meals in the myfitnesspal app, which we monitor daily. We'll then meet on a regular basis to take measurements, track progress, and address any challenges you encounter on your way to completing your SWEAT INTENSIVE.
