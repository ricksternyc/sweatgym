      Carl | Oakland Manager

      Carl manages the Oakland studio. He’s been in the fitness industry for over five years, and he loves sharing his passion of health with everyone he meets. He recently earned a master's degree in Sports Psychology – and likes to use these skills to motivate and inspire his clients to set and achieve their goals.

      Jason | Group & Private Training Instructor

      Jason has spent much of his entire life in and around a pool – first as a competitive swimmer, and now as a coach. He brings that same passion and discipline to his classes at Sweat, where clients say that his focus on consistency and proper form lead to extremely challenging and effective workouts.

      Jennifer | Group & Private Training Instructor

      Jenn has been in the fitness industry for five years, where she’s earned a reputation for her upbeat personality and high energy workouts. She also recently earned her Physical Therapy license from the State of California, and enjoys working with both traditional clients and those needing rehabilitative modifications.
